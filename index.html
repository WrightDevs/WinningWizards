Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the Winning Wizards community?

+

Joining our community is easy! Simply click the "Join Community" button and follow the registration process. You'll get access to tournaments, exclusive content, and our Discord server.

Are tournaments free to enter?

+

We offer both free and premium tournaments. Free tournaments are open to all community members, while premium tournaments have entry fees but offer larger prize pools and exclusive rewards.

What games do you support?

+

We support a wide variety of popular competitive games including League of Legends, Valorant, CS2, Fortnite, Apex Legends, and many more. Our tournament schedule covers different games throughout the week.

How do I participate in tournaments?

+

Once you're a community member, you can browse upcoming tournaments in your dashboard and register for the ones you're interested in. Make sure to check the requirements and rules for each tournament.

Can I stream my gameplay on your platform?

+

We encourage community members to stream their gameplay. You can apply to become a featured streamer, and we'll help promote your content across our social media channels.

Is there a mobile app available?

+

We're currently developing a mobile app that will be available for both iOS and Android. In the meantime, our website is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices.
